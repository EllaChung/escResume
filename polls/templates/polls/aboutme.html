What is the Purpose of This Site?

I created this site to practice my web development skills - through an interactive resume.

In creating this site, I aimed to apply what I learned in my CS courses, internships, online courses, etc. to a tangible project. The site is currently deployed through Heroku.

The 'Homepage' page welcomes you! Along with the navbar at the top, the homepage will allow you to navigate through this site.

The 'AboutMe' page, which you're already on, is where I've decided to introduce myself. See the 'Get in Touch!' box at the right for inquiries/questions/comments/etc. I've also listed my LinkedIn and Github as well.

The 'Resume' page further explains some of the skillsets that are on my resume. While LinkedIn has a quick summary of my resume, I wanted to add a more personal touch on my own site.

The 'Previous Work' page is like my portfolio - because Heroku only allows for 5 sites to be deployed for free, I've exhibited my work through screenshots, videos, or links.

The 'Course Work' page is a quick summary of my transcript. If you would like to view my full transcript to connect me with opportunities, please reach out to me on any of the contact information to the right of this message, under 'Get in Touch' or 'Contacts.'
